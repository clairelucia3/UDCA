General Fund

The UDCA General Fund has been established to support a wide range of community activities and initiatives. These include events such as the Memorial Day Picnic, Thanksgiving Fun Night, and the Lunar New Year Celebration and Chinese Culture Showcase, as well as programs like Student-Parent Forums and the UDCA Medal Awards and UDHS Outstanding Senior Awards. The fund also contributes to community-wide events like Upper Dublin Community Day and supports selected Upper Dublin School District student extracurricular activities and math clubs, helping to enrich educational and cultural experiences across the community.

Arts and Culture Fund

The Arts and Cultures Fund has been established to support the UD Intercultural Club, an initiative created in the early summer of 2022 to celebrate and share the community’s cultural diversity in collaboration with the Upper Dublin Public Library. Through this program, student volunteers and community leaders organize hands-on, art- and culture-based activities such as origami, paper cutting, painting, and calligraphy. The fund helps foster opportunities for individuals to connect through creative expression, with the goal of building deeper understanding, mutual respect, and meaningful relationships across cultures.
