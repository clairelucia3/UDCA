We have rebranded! We are still the same UDCA you know.
Welcome to the newly redesigned UDCA website! While the look and layout have been refreshed to create a more modern, accessible, and user-friendly experience, the heart of our organization remains the same. We are still committed to celebrating culture, supporting our community, and fostering meaningful connections across generations.

This updated design is meant to make it easier for you to explore our programs, stay informed about events, and engage with everything UDCA has to offer. Though the appearance may be new, our mission, values, and dedication to the community continue unchanged. We’re the same UDCA you know and trust, just with a fresh new look.

Celebrating Heritage, Building Community

The Upper Dublin Chinese American Association is an organization dedicated to celebrating Chinese culture by fostering communication and understanding among students and families in the Upper Dublin community through cultural events, educational programs, and community gatherings.
