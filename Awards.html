Awards

The Awards program recognizes individuals who demonstrate outstanding dedication, leadership, and service within the community. Through these honors, the association celebrates students, educators, and volunteers who make meaningful contributions and embody the values of excellence, initiative, and community impact.

UDCA Award

The UDCA Medal program and student volunteer award were inaugurated to encourage students in the UDCA community to pursue excellence in academics, extracurricular activities, leadership and community services. By recognizing those who have demonstrated outstanding achievements in those areas, we hope to show our high expectations for our children and promote well-rounded education and excellence for all.

Each Spring, the recipients are recognized during the UDCA Memorial Day Picnic event.

UDHS Senior Award

From 2016 onward, UDCA-sponsored scholarships have been awarded annually to two outstanding UDHS graduating seniors who have qualified as National Merit Finalists while maintaining excellent extracurricular performance records. The awardees are selected by the UDHS faculty and administrators.

Inspire Award

The INSPIRE award is sponsored by the Upper Dublin Chinese American Association and is awarded to an individual who consistently demonstrates the characteristics of an excellent educator, which include being Innovative, Nurturing, Spirited, Passionate, Inclusive, Resilient, and Empowering. The Recipient is announced at the annual Lunar New Year Gala.
